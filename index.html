Company information

Testemp Ltd is a UK based temperature sensor manufacturer and temperature instrumentation stockist with over 45 years' experience in supplying to the industry (initially plastics and rubber moulding). Testemp Ltd also stocks analogue temperature, digital temperature and power controllers. Our focus is on supplying high quality temperature sensors and temperature instrumentation products for the industrial market. Our current range of units includes thermocouples, platinum resistance, PT100, PT1000, thermistors, thermopockets, thermowells, transmitters, data loggers, compression fittings, bayonet adaptors, connectors, thermocouple cable and chart recorders.
